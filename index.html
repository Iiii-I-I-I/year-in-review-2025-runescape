2025 Year in Review

With the new year well on its way to starting, it's the perfect time to look back at the wiki in 2025 with our we're-trying-our-best-to-be-annual Year in Review! (The last one we did was in 2021; if you're interested, read it here.) Keep on reading to get a peek into the traffic, editing, and other notable trends that happened across the RuneScape Wikis in 2025.

As you scroll, you can use the buttons below to switch between statistics displayed for the RuneScape Wiki and the Old School RuneScape Wiki.

Traffic statistics

With RuneScape and OSRS growing more popular than ever last year, it should come as no surprise that the wikis have continued to grow as well. In 2025 we reached the 2,000,000,000 (that's billion, with a B!) pageview mark for the first time in a single year, with over 5 million visits per day on average!

As usual, wiki traffic trends closely follow major game updates and events, with our biggest peaks coming during the Grid Master event and around Sailing's release. Another notable bump appeared around Catalyst League, and as well as a fun summer upswell of streaming popularity.

Notable updates

Aggregated stats are always fun to look at, but some of the most interesting quirks of wiki traffic are the unexpected page spikes that accompany major updates. Take a look at these updates and which pages had their most popular traffic days in their wake — there's always at least one old page that has a resurgence in popularity, or an odd new addition that players really needed to know more about.

15 September 2025

Catalyst League

Besides the expected peaks such as the guide and relics pages, RuneScape's first league brought renewed interest in early game quests, which players flocked to for first time in years. Regular ghostly ink and other Necromancy materials also saw spikes during the league, potentially from players returning to the game for the league and needing to do some quick catching up on the newest skill.

Thank you!

2025 was a big year for RuneScape, with Old School RuneScape's first new skill and RuneScape's first league. With the Myreque finale The Blood Moon Rises, the sixth league Demonic Pacts, and Morytania expansion Havenhythe likely to arrive in the next few months, 2026 is already shaping up to be another banger of a year for both the game and for the wikis.

If you want to get involved, there's always work to do and new projects are being started all the time. If you haven't edited before and aren't sure where to start, join us in our Discord server.
